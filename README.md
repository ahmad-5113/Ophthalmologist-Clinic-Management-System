The Ophthalmology Clinic Management System is a database-driven application designed to enhance the efficiency of operations within an eye clinic. The system is built for two key stakeholders: Ophthalmologists and Patients. Ophthalmologists can use the platform to manage appointments, maintain patient records, track treatment history, and issue prescriptions. Patients can register, manage their accounts, book appointments, view their medical history, and provide feedback. The goal of this project is to create a digital solution that ensures organized data management, seamless communication, and convenient access to healthcare services.

User Types: 
User A: Ophthalmologist
User B: Patient
Functional Requirement 1: Register and Login (Mahad)
	The system shall allow Ophthalmologists and Patients to register an account and log in. 
Functional Requirement 2: Appointment Management (Rashad)
	The system shall allow the Ophthalmologist to accept, reject and reschedule appointments based on their availability. It shall allow Patients to book appointments by selecting their preferred Ophthalmologist and time slot.

Functional Requirement 4: Billing (Rashad) 
	The system shall allow the Ophthalmologist to track payments after the bill/invoice is generated, after the appointment is concluded.
Functional Requirement 5: Feedback (Mahad and Ahmed)
	The system shall allow the Patient to submit their feedback to the Ophthalmologist after every appointment.
